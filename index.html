ABOUT US

PORIGAMA is a UK-based company specializing in consulting and software development for the mobile gaming industry.

Led by professionals with years in mobile gaming, PORIGAMA stands out thanks to its deep domain knowledge and proven track record of delivering successful products on both iOS and Android platforms.

From internal analytics systems for performance tracking to full-cycle product development, PORIGAMA supports every stage of building world-class mobile games.

Our team combines technical excellence with creative vision to create engaging experiences that players love.
